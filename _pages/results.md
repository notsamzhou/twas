Out of 633 genes on chromosome 22, TWAS found 100 genes that are highly heritable with IBD, 7 of which had a significant association with IBD. Of the seven genes, 5 of them were very close together and shared the same best GWAS SNP, which might suggest that they contribute to the same signal.
